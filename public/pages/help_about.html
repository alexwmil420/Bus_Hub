How to Use BusHub

1. For Students

Create an account using your UB email address. Once logged in, you can submit a complaint through the Submit Complaint form, attach a description, and optionally include media. You can later track its status in your dashboard.

2. For Bus Drivers

View feedback relevant to your assigned route. Communicate with administrators regarding reported incidents and confirm resolution steps.

3. For Administrators

Log in to the Admin Dashboard to review and manage incoming reports. Update statuses (Pending, In Review, Resolved) and generate summary reports for the Transit Department.
